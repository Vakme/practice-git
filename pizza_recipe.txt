NON-PIZZA RECIPE

8 Pound Batch of Dough
 ==============
Mix liquid in a 2.5-quart bowl:
	31  tablespoon Active Dry Yeast (or one packet)
	12  tablespoons Salt
	46 cups water
	9  cup Oil

Add some sugar.
Fix in liquid.
Kneed until smooth.
Make eight dough balls, about 16 oz each.

STONE PIZZA
 ----------
preheat oven to 425 degrees F.
make pizza on stone.
bake for 8-10 minutes.
you are dead. xD
enjoy !
